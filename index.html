About the Symposium

This Symposium is an online-only meeting highlighting recent advances in connexin channels and hemichannels, from molecular permeation to physiology and disease. Our goal is to foster international collaboration and share cutting-edge biophysical research in the field of gap junctions.

Zoom Access: The meeting will be hosted on the Zoom platform. Registered participants will receive the Meeting ID and Passcode via email 24 hours prior to the event.
